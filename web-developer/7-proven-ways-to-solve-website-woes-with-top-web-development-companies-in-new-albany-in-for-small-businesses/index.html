You've invested time and resources into your small business website in New Albany, IN, and now you want to ensure you're getting the best return on that investment. Measuring the success of your website is crucial for understanding its impact on your business. Here's how you can do it:

  • Traffic Analysis: Use tools like Google Analytics to track your website's traffic. Look at metrics such as total visits, unique visitors, and page views to gauge interest.
  • Conversion Rates: Monitor how many visitors take the desired action, whether it's making a purchase, filling out a form, or subscribing to a newsletter. A high conversion rate indicates your website is effective.
